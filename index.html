Welcome to the Dark Crystal Collector Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below, clicking them will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number that is shown.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time the game starts the crystals will have different values.
